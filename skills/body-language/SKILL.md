# Body Language Analysis

## Quick Start
For any video source, analyze:
1. Baseline posture (relaxed state)
2. Stress responses (under pressure)
3. Confidence markers (ownership of space)
4. Baseline vs. peak comparison

## Signal Categories

| Category | What to Look For | Diagnostic Value |
|---|---|---|
| Baseline Posture | Relaxed shoulders, natural breathing, unguarded hands | Establishes truth baseline |
| Stress Responses | Shoulder tension, blink rate change, protective gestures | Reveals authentic discomfort |
| Confidence Markers | Open posture, steady gaze, deliberate movement | Indicates ownership of space |
| Micro-expressions | Fleeting emotional flashes (0.5-4 seconds) | Reveals suppressed affect |

## Access Level Impact
- **RAW sources** (BTS, leaked footage): Baseline is authentic
- **MANAGED sources** (podcasts, festivals): Cross-reference with RAW
- **SCRIPTED sources** (late night, press junkets): Only compare SCRIPTED-to-SCRIPTED

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Baseline established from at least 2 non-performance moments
- [ ] Stress responses mapped to specific timestamps
- [ ] Confidence markers verified across ≥2 sources
- [ ] Baseline vs. peak comparison completed
- [ ] All claims tagged with access level and tier
