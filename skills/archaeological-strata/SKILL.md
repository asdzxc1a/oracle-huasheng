# Archaeological Strata

## The Five Layers

| Layer | Time Period | What to Excavate | Diagnostic Value |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bedrock | Childhood / Formation | Family dynamics, early training, formative experiences | Reveals unchangeable core traits |
| Foundation | Breakthrough Era | First major success, how fame was initially handled | Shows baseline relationship with celebrity |
| Renovation | Recent Evolution | How success has changed behavior | Indicates adaptability or defensive growth |
| Turf | Current Cycle | Most recent public behavior | Current-state snapshot |
| Subsoil | Hidden / Private | What peers and collaborators say off-record | Reveals gap between public and private |

## Excavation Method
1. For each layer, find video evidence from that era
2. Compare baseline behaviors across layers
3. Identify what changed and what stayed stable
4. Assess whether changes are growth or defensive masking

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] At least 3 layers have video evidence
- [ ] Cross-era comparisons are explicit
- [ ] Stability vs. change is named for each trait
- [ ] All claims tagged with era and tier
