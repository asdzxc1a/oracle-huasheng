# Intelligence Fusion

## Assessment Matrix

| Dimension | What to Assess | Red Flag | Green Light |
|---|---|---|---|
| Career Strategy | Role selection patterns, franchise dependency | Every choice is safe / franchise-backed | Mix of risk and safety with clear growth arc |
| Risk Appetite | Willingness to fail publicly, non-franchise work | Never leaves safety net | Takes calculated risks with downside protection |
| Alliance Patterns | Director relationships, repeat collaborations | Jumps from project to project with no continuity | Builds long-term creative partnerships |
| Relationship to Fame | How celebrity is managed, privacy boundaries | Either addicted to attention or completely withdrawn | Manages fame as a tool, not an identity |
| Commercial Viability | Box office draw, audience engagement | Can't open a film without IP safety | Draws audience regardless of franchise |

## Fusion Rule
Intelligence assessment is not biography. It is strategic analysis of what
career choices reveal about the actor's psychology and future trajectory.

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] All five dimensions assessed
- [ ] Evidence is specific (named films, directors, years)
- [ ] Pattern identified, not just chronology listed
- [ ] All claims tagged with tier
