# The Slate Oracle

## Format
Portfolio intelligence brief (1,200-1,800 words)

## Audience
Studio head or financier evaluating a slate of projects

## Structure

### The Actor as Portfolio
How does this actor distribute risk across their filmography?
- Franchise safety net analysis
- Indie cred investments
- Genre spread
- Director-tier strategy

### The Gap Analysis
What is missing from their portfolio?
- What role type have they never played?
- What genre have they avoided?
- What director tier have they not reached?

### The Prediction
Where will they be in 3 years based on current trajectory?
- **Optimistic:** Everything current pays off
- **Realistic:** Base-case scenario
- **Pessimistic:** Current risks materialize

### The Investment Thesis
If you were buying stock in this actor's career:
- **Bull case:** Why invest heavily
- **Bear case:** Why stay cautious
- **Price:** What project budget range fits their current valuation?
