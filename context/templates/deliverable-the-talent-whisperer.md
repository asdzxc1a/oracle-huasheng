# The Talent Whisperer

## Format
One-page psychological portrait (500-700 words)

## Audience
Producer/director who trusts intuition but wants evidence

## Structure

### Opening Line
A single sentence that captures the actor's essence. Not a resume fact. An insight.

Example: *"She doesn't audition — she auditioned once, at 14, and has been proving that moment right ever since."*

### The Core Pattern (150 words)
What is the single behavioral pattern that predicts everything else?
- When stressed, do they fight or flee?
- What do they do when no one is directing them?
- What is their relationship to the camera when they think it's off?

### The Casting Question (200 words)
Direct answer to the client's specific question.
- Yes/No/Maybe with confidence level
- 2-3 pieces of video evidence supporting the answer
- 1 genuine risk factor (not a token concern — a real one)

### The Inflection Point (100 words)
What decision in the next 12-18 months will determine their trajectory?
- Franchise vs. indie?
- Safety vs. risk?
- Visibility vs. craft?

### The Verdict (50 words)
One paragraph, no hedging. The oracle speaks.
