# The Greenlight Confessor

## Format
Three-page confidential brief (1,500-2,000 words)

## Audience
Studio executive with $25M+ decision authority

## Structure

### Page 1: Executive Summary
- **Verdict:** Green / Yellow / Red with confidence percentage
- **Key Risk:** The single thing that could make this a disaster
- **Key Upside:** The single thing that could make this a triumph
- **Recommendation:** Proceed / Proceed with conditions / Pass

### Page 2: Evidence

#### Section A: Behavioral Profile
- Clinical observations from video evidence
- Stress response pattern
- Social calibration assessment
- Tier-marked claims (A/B/C)

#### Section B: Career Trajectory
- Archaeological strata analysis
- Comparable mapping (who succeeded/failed at this transition)
- Contradiction map (what doesn't fit the narrative)

#### Section C: Adversarial Analysis
- The strongest case against greenlighting
- What would make us regret this decision?
- What are we not seeing?

### Page 3: Decision Framework

#### Risk Scenarios
- **Best case:** What happens if everything goes right?
- **Base case:** Most likely outcome
- **Worst case:** What happens if the risk factor materializes?

#### Conditions (if Yellow)
- What would need to be true to upgrade to Green?
- What protections should be in the contract?

#### Final Verdict
The oracle's judgment. No hedging.
