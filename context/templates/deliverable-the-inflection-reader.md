# The Inflection Reader

## Format
Visual trajectory map + 1-page narrative (800-1,000 words)

## Audience
Agent or manager planning career strategy

## Structure

### The Trajectory Map
A visual timeline showing:
- **Bedrock era** (childhood/formation) — 1-2 key events
- **Foundation era** (breakthrough) — The role that changed everything
- **Renovation era** (current) — What is being built now
- **Projected fork** — Two possible futures based on next decision

### The Narrative

#### Where They Are
- Current stratum analysis
- What is working now and why
- What is not working and why

#### The Fork
Two paths diverge from the current moment:

**Path A (Safety):**
- What it looks like
- 3 comparable actors who took this path
- Outcome probability

**Path B (Risk):**
- What it looks like
- 3 comparable actors who took this path
- Outcome probability

#### The Oracle's Read
Which path aligns with their actual (not performed) identity?
- What does the video evidence say they really want?
- What are they pretending to want because they think they should?
- What would they choose if no one was watching?
