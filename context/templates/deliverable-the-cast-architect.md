# The Cast Architect

## Format
Ensemble design brief (1,000-1,500 words)

## Audience
Casting director or director designing an ensemble

## Structure

### The Anchor
The target actor's role in the proposed ensemble:
- What do they stabilize?
- What do they destabilize?
- What chemistry dynamics are predicted?

### Complementary Profiles
3-4 suggested co-stars with:
- **Why this pairing works:** Behavioral complementarity
- **Risk factor:** What could go wrong in the dynamic
- **Evidence:** Video clips showing compatible stress responses

### The Tension Map
Where will the on-set or on-screen tension come from?
- Power dynamics between characters
- Method differences between actors
- Predicted friction points and how to manage them

### The Oracle's Cast
If this were your film and your money, who would you cast around them?
- Not based on availability or budget
- Based purely on behavioral compatibility
