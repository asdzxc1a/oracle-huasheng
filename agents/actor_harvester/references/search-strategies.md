# Search Strategies — Actor Harvester

## YouTube Search Operators
- `"{actor} interview" after:2023` — Recent interviews
- `"{actor}" "press conference" "cannes"` — Festival Q&As
- `"{actor}" "behind the scenes"` — BTS footage
- `"{actor}" podcast` — Long-form conversations

## Podcast Platforms
- Spotify: Search actor name, filter by Episodes
- Apple Podcasts: Similar approach
- Look for industry podcasts: The Treatment, Happy Sad Confused, WTF with Marc Maron

## Social Media
- TikTok: `{actor} interview` — often clips from longer sources
- Instagram: Check official accounts for Stories/Reels (ephemeral, screenshot if critical)

## Festival Archives
- Cannes: festival-cannes.com (press conference transcripts)
- TIFF: tiff.net (video archives for premium members)
- Sundance: sundance.org (artist conversations)

## Source Reliability Tiers
1. **Official festival channels** — Highest reliability
2. **Verified journalist channels** — High reliability (IndieWire, Variety)
3. **Fan channels** — Moderate reliability, may have rare clips but also mislabeled content
4. **Reuploads** — Lowest reliability, often edited or misattributed
