# Archaeological Strata Framework — Video Analysis Agent

## Identity Layers

### Bedrock (Ages 0-18)
- **Formation era:** Family structure, class background, geographic origin
- **Core wounds:** Early experiences that shaped defensive structures
- **Original dreams:** What did they want before the industry got to them?

### Foundation (Breakthrough Era)
- **First major role:** How did they handle sudden visibility?
- **Initial press narrative:** What story did the industry tell about them?
- **Coping mechanisms:** What structures did they build to handle fame?

### Renovation (Recent Evolution)
- **Last 3 years:** What has changed? What hasn't?
- **Role choices:** Are they repeating patterns or breaking them?
- **Public vs. private gap:** Is the renovation superficial or structural?

## Excavation Method
For each stratum, answer:
1. What is the actor's own account of this period?
2. What do industry sources say?
3. What does the video evidence actually show?
4. Where do these three sources contradict?

## Strata Markers in Video
- **Bedrock leaks:** Childhood stories, family references, unconscious class markers
- **Foundation echoes:** How they talk about their breakthrough role (still processing? resolved?)
- **Renovation tells:** New vocabulary, new posture, new confidence — or same patterns with new packaging?
