# Body Language Analysis Framework — Video Analysis Agent

## Observational Categories

### Baseline Behavior
- How does the actor sit/stand when comfortable?
- What is their default facial expression?
- How do they use their hands when not stressed?

### Stress Indicators
- **Micro-expressions:** Fleek emotions that leak through (Ekman framework)
- **Adapter behaviors:** Touching face, neck, hair (self-soothing)
- **Posture shifts:** Leaning away, closed body language
- **Blink rate changes:** Rapid blinking under cognitive load

### Engagement Signals
- **Eye contact patterns:** Sustained vs. darting
- **Head tilts:** Indicates interest or submission
- **Mirroring:** Unconscious imitation of interviewer (rapport signal)
- **Forward lean:** Engagement and interest

### Emotional Range Markers
- **Volatility:** How quickly does expression change?
- **Recovery time:** How fast after a stressor does baseline return?
- **Asymmetry:** Genuine smiles (Duchenne) vs. social smiles

## Scoring Matrix
For each video, rate 1-5:
| Dimension | 1 (Low) | 5 (High) |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Emotional Expressiveness | Flat/monotone | Rich, rapid transitions |
| Stress Resilience | Collapses under pressure | Maintains composure |
| Social Calibration | Awkward, mismatched | Perfectly calibrated |
| Physical Presence | Shrinks, disappears | Commands space effortlessly |
