# Voice Analysis Framework — Video Analysis Agent

## Acoustic Features

### Pitch & Register
- **Baseline pitch:** Where does the voice naturally sit?
- **Pitch range:** How much variation under emotion?
- **Vocal fry / creak:** Indicator of fatigue, relaxation, or affectation

### Tempo & Rhythm
- **Speech rate:** Words per minute at baseline vs. under stress
- **Pause patterns:** Strategic pauses vs. frozen silence
- **Interruptions:** Does the actor interrupt? Get interrupted?

### Timbre & Quality
- **Resonance:** Chest voice (grounded) vs. head voice (thin)
- **Breathiness:** Intimacy signal or vocal strain?
- **Harshness:** Anger, determination, or vocal damage?

### Emotional Markers
- **Prosody:** Melodic variation (flat = low engagement, varied = high)
- **Volume dynamics:** Whispers for intimacy, projection for authority
- **Tremor:** Nervousness or genuine emotion?

## Intelligence Indicators
- **Vocabulary range:** Latinate vs. Anglo-Saxon word choice
- **Syntactic complexity:** Sentence structure sophistication
- **Hesitation markers:** "ums" and "uhs" as cognitive load indicators
- **Self-correction rate:** How often do they revise their own statements?
